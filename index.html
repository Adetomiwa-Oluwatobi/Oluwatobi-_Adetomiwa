Real-Time Job Application Notification & Reporting System

**Real-Time Job Application Notification & Reporting System** A smart automation workflow designed to help HR teams stay informed and responsive throughout the hiring process. The system sends instant Slack notifications whenever a new job application is received, including key applicant details and a direct link to the Google Sheet entry for quick access and follow-up. It also generates AI-powered daily summaries that categorize applications by job role and provide a clear breakdown of total applications received. These reports are automatically saved to Google Sheets for easy tracking and reference. By combining real-time alerts with automated reporting, the workflow improves visibility, speeds up response times, and streamlines recruitment management. Use Case Impact: HR teams now have instant visibility into new applicants and a daily snapshot of all applications by role. This enables faster, more accurate, and fully automated tracking, reporting, and decision-making.
